Four elements of the Clover OS structure

Back End

Functions

01
The Clover Back End connects to the Polkadot ecosystem through slots, sharing Polkadot's security and consensus.
02
The Clover Back End supports various Defi basic protocols and tools.

Development

01
The Clover chain and basic protocols are developed by first-party developers.
02
Extension protocols and basic tools are developed by second-party developers.

Front End

Functions

01
The Clover Front End, through Clover Kernel technology, realizes the adaptation and operation of multiple OSs.
02
The user interface includes user dApp entrance, user wallet, and one-stop asset explorer.

Development

01
The Clover Kernel and User Interface are developed by first-party developers.

High Level Smart Contract

Functions

01
The Clover High Level Smart Contract, through Tulip technology, realizes one-stop development and cross-chain deployment of Smart Contract.
02
Tulip language, basic development tools, and development documents are implemented by first-party developers.
03
SC developers, based on Clover development tools, develop a variety of dApps with a lower cost.
04
One-click deployment reduces the deployment cost of rented network, storage, and bandwidth.

Clover OS Storage

Development

01
Tulip and development tools are developed by first-party developers.
02
dApps are developed and operated by third-party developers.
